Why 3D tours are the future of hotel marketing

The biggest barrier to booking is uncertainty. Guests ask themselves: Is the room as nice as the photos? Is the pool actually usable? What does the view look like? A 3D virtual tour eliminates all of this uncertainty. The guest walks through your hotel virtually, falls in love with it — and books with confidence.

Hotels with 3D tours on their Booking.com and website listings see an average 16% increase in conversion rate. On Google Maps, properties with virtual tours receive 2x more engagement than those without.
